What is a Creative Tensions event?

 

Creative Tensions is an ongoing series of hosted conversations that foster open dialogue on challenging topics. It’s not a panel; it’s a conversation that moves. Reflecting on a tension between two poles, participants reveal where they stand on an issue by where they stand—and move—in the room.

 

To learn more about Creative Tensions, or to attend an upcoming event, visit www.creativetensions.com.

Creative Tensions is produced in partnership by IDEO, a global design firm committed to creating positive impact in the world through design, and the Sundance Institute Theatre Program, which advances the work of risk-taking theatre artists by providing rare developmental opportunities that support artists throughout their careers.
